Cambridge University Botanic Garden

Since its opening in 1846, Cambridge University Botanic Garden has been an inspiration for gardeners, an exciting introduction to the natural world for families and a refreshing oasis for visitors. The heritage-listed Garden has been designed for both year-round interest and seasonal inspiration with plantings that intrigue and enchant.
