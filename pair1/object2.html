Polar Museum

The Scott Polar Research Institute was founded in 1920 by the young geologist Frank Debenham. With Debenham's encouragement, many explorers and their families donated objects associated with Polar expeditions. The Polar Museum continues to collect Polar equipment, scientific data, personal accounts and visual records, as well as the art and sculpture of the people of the circumpolar North.
