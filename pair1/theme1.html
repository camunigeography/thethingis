Geological Specimens

Catalogue of Geological Specimens from the Terra Nova Expedition

These pages come from the catalogue of geological specimens collected during the British Antarctic (Terra Nova) Expedition 1910–13, led by Captain Robert Falcon Scott. The work of conducting geological surveys was as central to this early expedition as to those on which Peter Clarkson collected his specimens and continues to play a key role in polar science. (Images courtesy of the Sedgwick Museum)
