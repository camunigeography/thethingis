Museum of Archaeology & Anthropology

The Museum of Archaeology and Anthropology in Cambridge has one of the most important collections of its kind in the UK. Originally founded in 1884, it has been in its present location since 1913. The museum's collections span nearly two million years of human history, on all six inhabited continents.
